Sam Portman, Research Collaborator. images/icons/genilab-perception.png Understanding how our academic community perceives their confidence, skills, and maturity with Generative AI. Our objective is to understand how our community engages with Generative Intelligence Systems both in practice and perception. We examine how these tools are used across disciplines, what influences adoption, and how educational programs can evolve to build GenAI literacy. * How confident are individuals in using Generative AI tools for learning, teaching, and research? * What are the perceived barriers and motivations influencing the adoption of GenAI in academic contexts? * How does perceived skill maturity vary across disciplines and experience levels? * What opportunities exist to enhance awareness, training, and integration of Generative Intelligence Systems in academia? The study uses survey-based data collection combined with qualitative analysis of participant responses and activities. Take the Survey; https://forms.gle/zcW1jFdB7CBRBnvj7
