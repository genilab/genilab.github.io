= The Research Framing Companion Dr. Fernando Koch /images/projects/research-bot.png A companion intelligence for validating research ideas and shaping academically grounded proposal framing. This project examines how companion intelligence can support early-career researchers in structuring and framing research proposals in an academically grounded manner. We provide a GPT-based companion that guides users through the required proposal sections while enforcing strict operational guidelines that preserve academic integrity and prevent automated authorship. We aim to analyze how users perceive and engage with such companion intelligence when developing and refining creative research ideas.
