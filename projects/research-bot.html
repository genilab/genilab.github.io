= The Research Framing Companion Dr. Fernando Koch /images/projects/research-bot.png A companion intelligence for early validation of research directions. This project studies companion intelligence for research proposal development. We provide a GPT-based assistant that guides users through common proposal components. The interaction operates following guardrails to preserve academic integrity. The study examines user engagement and behavioral imapct, including preceived benefits, risksm overreliance, and inflated confidence.
