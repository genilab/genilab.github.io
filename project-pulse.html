Hosted by The Generative Intelligence Lab and Adams Center for Entrepreneurship. images/icons/genilab-business.png How Local Businesses are Thinking About or Using Generative AI to improve their business operations? Our objective is to understand the current utilization of Generative intelligence Systems by local business ecosystems, exploring the following questions: * How they are preparing to assimilate AI? * What are the priority demands? * What are the main deficiencies? * How can we help? We are interview with small and midsize business applying a structured research process, curating a dataset of relevant research data, and releasing research reports.
