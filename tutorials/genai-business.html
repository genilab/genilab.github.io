Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at FAU /images/icons/genilab-knowledge.png WORKSHOP - Generative Intelligence and Business This workshop (2-days) provides a deep dive on Generative Intelligence and how it is transforming the way organizations design, execute, and scale business processes. Participants will learn the core concepts behind GenAI and agentic AI, explore real-world workflows and case studies, and leave with repeatable frameworks for moving from experimentation to AI-first and ultimately AI-native operating models. * What is Generative Intelligence, and how does it differ from traditional AI used in business? * How can startups and entrepreneurs use GenAI to ideate, prototype, and launch new products or services? * How do you redesign a workflow to become AI-first? * How do you design agents that can plan, use tools, collaborate, and remain safe and auditable? * How do leaders manage adoption: operating model, change management, and responsible use? * How can non-technical founders leverage GenAI to build interactive tools?

Topics

* Understanding Generative Intelligence * Where GenAI Creates Business Value * Designing GenAI Workflows * Case Studies of GenAI-centered business workflows * Fundamentals of Agentic AI * Building Agentic Workflows * Case Studies for Agentic AI in Business * Fundamentals of AI-Driven Organizations * Becoming AI-Native * Research & Case Studies
