Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at FAU /images/icons/genilab-knowledge.png TALK - How to Use GenAI in Learning? This 1-hour talk introduces students to Generative Intelligence and Large Language Models from a learning-centered perspective. * What is Generative AI, and how does it differ from traditional AI and search-based systems? * How do Large Language Models work at a conceptual level, and what can they realistically support in learning? * How does the level of automation influence understanding, performance, and skill development? * How can GenAI be used to support metacognition and context expansion?

Topics

* Generative AI and Learning. * Making the Best Use of GenAI. * Applying the Right Voice. * Turning GenAI into a Study Partner.

Target Audience
