Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at FAU images/icons/genilab-knowledge.png TUTORIAL - Generative Intelligence and Business This tutorial (1-2 hours) introduces Generative Intelligence and how it is reshaping business processes. * What is Generative Intelligence, and how does it differ from traditional AI used in business? * How can startups and entrepreneurs use GenAI to ideate, prototype, and launch new products or services? * How can non-technical founders leverage GenAI to build interactive tools like chatbots, customer assistants, or internal helpers that make sense in business contexts?

Target Audience

This talk is intended for faculty, business leaders, and students from non-technical backgrounds who are interested in exploring the role of Generative Intelligence in business.

Proposed Structure

* Topic 1 - Understanding Generative Intelligence: What Generative AI is and why it matters in business. * Topic 2 - GenAI in Business Applications: What Generative AI can and cannot do in business settings. * Topic 3 - Interacting better with Generative Intelligence: Basic view of prompt engineering and how to write clear and simple instructions to get useful, relevant results. * Topic 4 - Agentic AI: Introduction to agents and how they differ from simple tools. * Topic 5 - AI-Driven Organizations: Understanding when AI systems act as the core operational in business operations.
